THINGS YOU WILL NEED

  • 18 t-Shirts
  • 1 Rotary Cutter (such as the Fiskars 45mm Contour Rotary Cutter)
  • A sewing machine
  • Thread
  • Straight pins
  • A 13 inch by 13 inch square cardboard template
  • Backing fabric (such as flannel)
 

HOW TO MAKE THE QUILTS

  1. Cut the t-shirts into 36 13inch by 13 inch squares. The easiest way to do this is to lay the T-shirt flat, place the template on the shirt and use a rolling knife use back and front at the same time.
  2. Use the straight pins to connect the t-shirt squares (leave a ½ inch overlap on each T-shirt).
  3. Connect and sew 6 squares in a row
  4. Create 6 rows
  5. Connect and sew the rows of squares to each other using the same method at #3
  6. Add backing
  7. When the quilts are complete mail them to the HOMELOST project.
